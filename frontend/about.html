Why this exists

Hole in the Wall is designed to help students quickly find affordable food options while helping local and growing businesses reach the communities around them.

Student side

Browse food deals, study-friendly spots, catering options, and fundraiser-friendly restaurants near campus.

School side

Partner with the platform to support students and eventually verify campus communities through education emails.

Restaurant side

Claim a page, promote student offers, and share your story so your business stands out with heart and context.

Claimed vs unclaimed pages

Your platform can seed pages with public listing data. Then owners can claim those pages, verify details, and publish official deals, photos, and fundraiser or catering offers.
